About the Project

The Community Alarm Alert System is designed to improve safety and emergency response within communities. It enables quick alerts during emergencies like fire, theft, or accidents, ensuring everyone is informed and can respond quickly.

Key Features

  • Instant alerts via sirens, SMS, or notifications
  • Easy-to-use interface for all community members
  • Affordable and accessible technology
  • Encourages cooperation during emergencies
